About me

Personal Statement

My name is Junjie Hua. I am a current Ph.D student majoring in Bioinformatics Technology at Osaka University @Osaka,Japan.

[Top]

My dream is to make people have a virtual thermal perception through robot(s) or artificail arm(s). With the help of virtual thermal perception, in the future, we may be

  1. able to work at home or somewhere safe, distant from dangerous working environment while using a robot work for us.

  2. able to work with more than two hands for ordinary people, and be able to work like ordinary people for disabled people by using artificial arm(s).

  3. able to take one's temperature remotely and thus avoid the risk of being infected.

Research Experience

I did research about "Thermal illusion induced by recording and reproduce of technology of electrical stimulation" from 2017-2019. Current research theme is about "Thermal slope illusion and the spatial frequency and temperature difference characteristics".
